Tony Weakley Stonemasonry

Tony Weakley Stonemasonry is a business website for the client's 50 year stonemasonry business.

The client needed a site that would highlight his work and craftmanship. With this in mind, we decided to showcase his work in a gallery making it the first thing the viewer sees.

We follow up with the introduction statement, inroducing the audience to the man behind the business. Next, we add the list of services, so the visiter and potential customer knows the type of work the client can do. Then we added a "What We Do" section, highlighting the quality of his work, the stone used, and attention to detail in each project. We wrapped up the site with a footer that provides the client's contact information.

We decided to use a responsive, single-column, single-page site to make it easier for the client to maintain and update periodically.

You can visit the website at weakleystonemasonry.com
