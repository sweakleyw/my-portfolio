Derek Jeter Infographic

Derek Jeter became a Hall of Fame baseball player, due to his successful career.

The challenge of the graphic was to create an infographic that is easy to digest, while highlighting his awards and statistics over the course of his career that got him to the Hall of Fame.

The focus was to highlight his awards as illustrations and his statistics with different graphs. The illustrations help to drive the point of how many awards he actually won, while the graphs show how great his hit record and other statistics were.

The colors, pinstripes, and number 2 are elements he was associated with over his career. While the white silhouette is of him celebrating his last walk-off RBI of his career.
