Creepy Crochet

The client wanted a spooky-esque logo for their crochet business.

The client is a horror fan and makes a variety of spooky products. The products are more cutesy-scary than gore and horror, and they wanted the logo to represent that.

The type was hand drawn and rendered. It is a friendlier spooky font, with bubbly “getting chills” font. The 'O' in crochet is shaped as a coffin to emphasize the theme.

The client also asked for bats, because bats are their favorite animal and continue to fit the spooky theme.

As for colors, the client loves and wanted purple. Also with the platform they were looking to use to sell, the two tone purple was influenced by it.
