The Total Look

The client wanted an elegant logo for their hair salon The Total Look.

The client wanted a logo where the scissors in the background were shut, and the name of the salon was in the front with a script font.

The client has an elegant salon with pink walls, with accents of gold throughout. She wanted a logo with a similar color palette, where some colors could be alternated depending on printing (banners, business cards) or digital (social media graphics).

There was a preference for a pink background on different products, so the letters are white while the scissors are gold.
