Foggy River Cabins

The client wanted a logo for their cabin rental company.

The cabins are located in the Shenandoah Valley, by the river, nestled in the Blue Ridge Mountains.

They wanted the logo to feature the cabin in the mountains with trees, with the name Foggy River Cabins below.

The logo is by a geometric design with the cabin roof lined up with the mountains and the roof connecting to the tree line and base of the mountains.

For the color palette, the cabins are very distinct with the orange wooden siding and green roof. We kept with the natural color theme by using the same green for the trees, and chose a grey-blue color for the mountains. The mountain would have been overwhelming with a strong blue color like the mountain, so we toned it down.

The type across the bottom is an outdoors/western type of decorative style to fit the business.
