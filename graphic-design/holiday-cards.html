Holiday Cards

The holiday cards were made as a present from myself to my family and friends.

The intention of my cards was to highlight different holiday elements in a flat style, with a non-traditional color palette.

As a result of my color palette and flat design style, my decisions resulted in ornaments, a wreath, a Christmas tree in a snow globe, and snowflakes.

The non-traditional palette worked with each holiday element to give the vibes of the season, without screaming Christmas.
